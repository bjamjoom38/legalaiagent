Article 80: An employer may terminate an employee without end-of-service benefits if the employee commits a serious offense or violates the terms of their contract.
Article 74: A contract may be terminated by mutual consent between both parties.
Article 81: An employee may leave without notice if the employer fails to meet contractual obligations or exposes the employee to harm.
Article 37: Employment contracts for non-Saudis must be in writing and have a specified duration.
Article 77: If a party terminates a contract without valid reason, compensation must be paid.
